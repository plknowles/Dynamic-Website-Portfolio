My background

I was born in Hong Kong, and grew up living around the world, including the UK, Taiwan, and China. I was always interested in programming and technology as a child; I’d find ways of jailbreaking into phones and consoles, set up a halo gaming LAN in the school computer room and hosted my own website at 11. I didn’t initially go down the route of computing: my bachelor’s degree was in biomedical science and following that, I worked in operations management for two years at Amazon.

Starting in 2023, I decided to pursue a career in software development, starting an MSc in Computing at Cardiff University. The programme is a conversion course and hence fast-paced and challenging. I’ve had the opportunity to learn multiple languages such as SQL, JavaScript, python, HTML and CSS. I’ve also been dabbling with Java in my free time, creating a couple of GUI programs. I created this portfolio as part of a university coursework; however, I intend build on it further and use it to showcase all the projects I undertake in the following months and years. I’m at the beginning of my programming journey and working towards becoming a full stack developer, so I do expect there will be much more to come!

Personal and hobbies

I’m a friendly, open-minded person. I love travelling and enjoy trying a variety of new experiences – the more exciting it is, the more likely I’ll want to try it. I value my fitness and in my free time I regularly train indoor bouldering, calisthenics, and I’ll go on the occasional long hike. I’m interested in martial arts – particularly boxing, BJJ, and MMA; I train boxing a few times a week and regularly watch MMA and boxing events.

Outside of fitness, I do a lot of coding in my free time for fun. I like to do some casual photography on the side, and I can play two instruments, the violin and the piano. Finally, I can also ride a motorcycle and have a pilot’s licence!
